Salary to Hourly Calculator

Convert your annual salary into an hourly rate

How this works

This calculator divides your annual salary by the total number of hours you work in a year to estimate your hourly wage.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do you calculate hourly pay from salary?

We divide your annual salary by total working hours per year, adjusted for taxes and PTO.

Does this include taxes?

Yes. Enter an estimated tax rate to see your real take-home hourly pay.
